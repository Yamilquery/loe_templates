Welcome to LandOnEarth. Let’s get started right away saving you time and energy while providing outstanding service to your clients! LandOnEarth is your secret advantage to close more homes and stand out from the competition.

Now that your subscription is activated, you might be asking, what’s next?

In your account, you have a personalized invite link to give your clients access to LandOnEarth instantly. This link can be sent via email, text, social, or the many ways in which you communicate with your clients.

Step 1: Send your LandOnEarth invite link out to your client base. Give them instant access to start finding property matches today.

Step 2: When your clients activate their LandOnEarth access, they will be sent a Welcome email with steps to get their account set up and how to fill out their matching profile.

Step 3: Once your clients activate their account and start matching, you will be notified and able to see their saved home matches in real-time. See all of your active clients via your dashboard and say hello to the only tool you’ll ever need to help your clients find their perfect home match.
